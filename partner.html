Location Partner

For businesses and landowners who host a node: you're not donating money, you're donating something better — a home for the node. Your property becomes a small piece of public infrastructure. Nothing about hosting costs you anything ongoing:

  • No liability — the node is fully autonomous and self-powered.
  • No power draw from your building or meter.
  • No modification to any structure you own.
  • Removable without a trace, whenever you want it gone.

In return, your name goes on the enclosure as a founding Location Partner, on the community's poster line, and you get the ceremonial first ping when your node goes live — the moment it actually joins the mesh.
