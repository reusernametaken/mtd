Vail Corridor

Test path, but further along than the others in DEV/OPS: — there's already a real thread here, not just a brochure line.

The original pitch: a potential connection southward over the Gore Range to the Vail Mountain area, extending coverage into Eagle County for interested partners. That's still the shape of it. What's changed is there's now an actual contact: Andrew, ColoradoMesh's western-slope lead, has connected this project to SkyHubLink, whose co-owner has offered comms-building access on Vail Mountain itself — a tower with real elevation and real infrastructure already in place, not a bare ridgeline that needs a build from scratch.

Nothing's confirmed or scheduled yet. But of everything in DEV/OPS: right now, this is the one closest to being a real next step instead of a someday one.
