Who's Building This

Mesh That Divide is being designed and bootstrapped by a disabled resident of Kremmling, Colorado — a mesh networking hobbyist and radio tinkerer — with zero corporate backing, zero grants, and a ThinkPad.

Every node placement gets verified against CalTopo elevation profiles before it goes anywhere near a map. Every ridgeline under consideration is BLM or Forest Service land. Every build is costed down to the component, out loud, in public, before a dollar is asked for.

This project belongs to disability solidarity, rural solidarity, and the plain human right to be heard — wherever you happen to live, however far you are from the nearest bar of signal.
