Blue River Valley

Placeholder test path — a real one, though: once the Highway 40 corridor is established, the Blue River is the most natural southward extension.

The Blue River runs north from the Summit County ski areas down to its confluence with the Colorado River at Kremmling — right at the corridor's own western anchor. Connecting this drainage would extend the mesh south into some of Colorado's most heavily used recreation terrain, and it shares a literal endpoint with kNET already.
